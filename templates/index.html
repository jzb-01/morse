Whats C.O.W?

C.O.W (Continuous Online Wave) is your all-in-one Morse code companion, offering:

  • Translation – Convert text to Morse and vice versa effortlessly.
  • Telegraph Practice – Use a realistic simulator to refine timing and rhythm.
  • Koch Method Training – Learn Morse code step by step with a structured approach.
